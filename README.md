# Delivery-Performance-Analytics-Report-
A Power BI dashboard project that analyzes delivery performance, customer behavior, shipping efficiency, and product trends using a dataset of 4,922 orders. This report provides insights across regions, segments, products, and shipping modes to support data-driven decision-making and operational improvements.
 
### Power BI Dashboard – End-to-End Analysis

---

##  1. Problem Statement

The delivery operations team needed a centralized, data-driven solution to understand delivery performance across key business metrics such as:

- Total sales value  
- Order frequency  
- Customer purchase behavior  
- Shipping efficiency  
- Product and segment contributions  

Insights were previously scattered across spreadsheets, making it difficult to identify trends or operational issues.

**This dashboard was created to:**

- Track delivery performance and customer orders over time  
- Identify high-value regions, segments, and products  
- Monitor shipping efficiency and delivery speed  
- Understand customer purchase patterns  
- Support strategic decision-making

##  5. Insights

### Customer & Revenue Insights
- Consumer segment = strongest revenue generator  
- California & New York = high-value markets  
- A small number of loyal customers generate many orders → loyalty program recommended  

### Operational Insights
- Avg shipping time (4 days) can be optimized  
- Standard Class is most profitable & should remain prioritized  
- First Class & Same Day need review  

### Product Insights
- High-selling products are office items: staples, envelopes, binders  
- Seasonal demand peaks should guide stock planning  

---

##  6. Recommendations

###  Improve Shipping Efficiency
- Reduce avg shipping from **4 → 3 days**  
- Improve First Class & Same Day adoption  

###  Strengthen High-Value Regions
- Targeted campaigns in California, New York, Texas  
- Consider micro-delivery hubs  

###  Boost Consumer Segment Engagement
- Personalized promotions  
- Loyalty/VIP program  
- Bundle offers (office essentials)  

###  Product Strategy Improvements
- Increase inventory for top-demand items  
- Evaluate underperforming products  
- Prepare stock for peak months  

###  Leverage Customer Buying Patterns
- Major promotions during **March** and **Sept–Nov**  
- Forecast demand using historical trends  

---

##  7. Summary

This Power BI dashboard provides an interactive and comprehensive view of delivery performance.  
It reveals:

- Consumer segment drives the most revenue  
- California & New York are top-performing states  
- Standard Shipping is the most relied-on delivery mode  
- Product sales are dominated by everyday office supplies  
- Shipping speed can be improved to enhance customer satisfaction  

Overall, the dashboard provides a strong foundation for business decision-making, operational optimization, and customer/market strategy.
